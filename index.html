Study Caveats

1
Gold ≠ Silver
Silver’s industrial demand (solar panels, EVs) introduces supply/demand shocks that gold does not face.
2
USD ≠ CAD
A Canadian investor can get a different experience once FX is included.
3
Time Horizon Limitation — 20-Year Data Bias
A 20-year dataset may reduce relevance by averaging across outdated market regimes that no longer reflect current conditions.
5
Only Four Safety Criteria — Too Few for Definitive Judgment
Four criteria are too few for definitive judgment. Many other factors are ignored: storage costs, bid-ask spreads, liquidity risk, regulatory risk, tax treatment, currency hedging costs, and broader macroeconomic regimes. Changing the analysis period will materially shift all reported metrics.
